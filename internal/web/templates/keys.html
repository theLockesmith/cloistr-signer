    Add Proxy Key

    Connect to an upstream signer to sign as their identity. This enables signer chaining - you can sign as a business account while keeping your personal signer.

    Two ways to get this:
    1. With secret: Get a bunker:// URI from upstream's "Connect" button (auto-approves)
    2. Without secret: Use bunker://<upstream-pubkey>?relay=<relay-url> and ask the admin to "Grant Access" to your pubkey
